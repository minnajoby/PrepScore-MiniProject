About PrepScore

PrepScore is a smart web application designed to solve a critical problem for students and recent graduates: the uncertainty of their job market readiness. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic accomplishment and industry expectation.

Our platform allows you to build a comprehensive digital profile of your skills, education, and experiences. We then use our smart algorithm to analyze this data and provide you with a simple, powerful "Career Readiness Score."

How It Works

1. Build Your Comprehensive Profile

Easily add all your professional details in one place. Our intuitive forms for skills, education, certifications, and work experience make it simple to create a profile that's far more detailed than a traditional resume.

2. Receive Your Instant PrepScore

The moment you add or update your profile, our smart algorithm instantly analyzes the data. It generates your unique Career Readiness Score, giving you a clear benchmark of your current standing.

3. Get Actionable Feedback

PrepScore provides personalized suggestions on what skills to learn, projects to build, and certifications to earn. Use this targeted advice to improve your score and become a more competitive candidate.
